About Me

Welcome to My Weather Forecast Site

Hello! My name is Igor Rys, and I am the creator of this weather forecast website. My goal is to provide you with
accurate, up-to-date, and easily accessible weather information for the next four days,
along with some of the latest news to keep you informed.

Why I Built This Site

I’ve always been passionate about technology and how it can make our daily lives easier.
As someone who often plans activities based on the weather, I realized the need for a simple,
intuitive platform that not only forecasts weather but also provides related news. This site is a culmination of my interests in:

  • Web development:

    I enjoy building websites that are both functional and visually appealing.

  • Meteorology:

    Understanding weather patterns has always fascinated me.

  • User experience:

    I want to create tools that genuinely help people.

What You’ll Find Here

On this site, you’ll find:

  • 4-Day Weather Forecast:

    Accurate predictions for your area and beyond.

  • Top News:

    A curated selection of stories to keep you updated.

  • Easy Navigation:

    An intuitive interface to quickly find what you need.

My Mission

My mission is to provide a reliable and user-friendly platform that helps you plan your days with confidence.
Whether you’re deciding what to wear, planning a trip, or just curious about the weather, this site is here to assist you.
